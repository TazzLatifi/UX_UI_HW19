My Work

Project Summary

Ciana’s goal is to prevent the marginalization of new immigrants and refugees and facilitate their successful integration in their new country - The need for motivated volunteers is integral for the success of this NGO. My research findings were that the website lacks the structure and visual presence to effectively represent their goals and needs for those that are interested in becoming their advocates through volunteering or fundraising. The project requires improvements to the navigation, logistics and branding of the website so that the users can understand the goals while easily navigating the structural interface of the site. The users should feel engaged, excited, and easily understand the steps they can take to become volunteers or become involved in order to help with the cause and to carry out Ciana’s mission in helping immigrant communities through the volunteering process.

My Work

CIANA User Research

When this project was started we made the assumption that our users were the immigrants looking for legal and educational support. -I chose to keep the initial survey general and have the interviewees guide us. The questions I asked were definitely more focused on the immigrant community using the site - see question below and on graphic: -What does the organization do and what is the scope of their work and if that information was obvious when looking through the site. -Asked our users to find out information about assistance and the types of services they offer. - Asked our users to look at the process of donation and what the getting involved / volunteer opportunities looked like and to describe the opportunities. -My final question during my initial interview series of the users was would they recommend the site to new immigrants…. As you will see in the interview documentation we learned the assumption that the immigrants were the users was inaccurate.
